This is the primary page for web development regarding evaluation 1 section 2; now we will address the plot, essential details, team members, and reviews of Valorant.

Valorant is a free-to-play first-person shooter (FPS) that is both developed and released by Riot Games, the company renowned for their hugely popular MOBA, League of Legends. Valorant represents Riot's first big foray into the FPS genre, and it soon gained interest for having old-school tactical shooting mechanics combined with more contemporary character-based gameplay.
